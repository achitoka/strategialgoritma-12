Westerland

[Number 49 - 67] Western Shootin' Gallery, Country Bear Theater, Mark Twain Riverboat, Big Thunder Mountain, Cowboy Cookhouse.

Adventureland

[Number 23 - 48] Nature and cultural diversity world of adventure. Pirates of the Caribbean, Jungle Cruise, Enchanted Tiki Room, Swiss Family Treehouse, Western River Railroad, Cafe Orleans.

Fantasyland

[Number 74 - 103] A world of fairytales and dreams. Peter Pan's Flight, Snow White's Adventures, Cinderella's Fairy Tale Hall, "It's a Small World", Alice's Tea Party, Enchanted Tale of Beauty and the Beast, Pinocchio's Daring Journey, Pooh's Hunny Hunt.

Tomorrowland

[Number 120 - 137] A land of tomorrow where science and technology are in harmony. Space Mountain, Buzz Lightyear's Astro Blasters, Star Tours, Monsters, Inc. Ride & Go Seek, etc.

World Bazaar

[Number 1 - 22] A shopping center. It opens World Bazaar Confectionery, Penny Arcade, House of Greetings, Grand Emporium, etc.

Toontown

[Number 104 - 119] Mickey's House, Minnie's House, Roger Rabbit's Car Toon Spin, Gadget's Go Coaster, Donald's Boat.

Critter Country

[Number 68 - 73] Splash Mountain, Beaver Brothers Explorer Canoes, Grandma Sara's Kitchen, etc.
